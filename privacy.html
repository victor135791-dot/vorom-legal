Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

TL;DR: VOROM is on-device only. No ads. No trackers. No VOROM account. No server upload. No data resale. Your workout history stays on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

What VOROM stores locally

VOROM stores the data you create inside the app — workout logs, exercise catalog edits, body weight, biological sex and birth year/month (used to calculate heart-rate zones and strength benchmarks), training-mode settings, plates, custom images, and app settings. This data is stored locally on your device. VOROM does not operate an app account or server-sync system in the current local-only version.

Where your data lives

Your complete VOROM training database lives in the app's local SwiftData store on your device. iPhone ↔ Apple Watch workout sync uses Apple's WatchConnectivity framework between your own devices. VOROM does not upload your workouts, templates, exercises, heart-rate samples, GPS routes, distance traces, body weight, or app settings to VOROM-operated servers in the current local-only version.

HealthKit

If you grant Health permission, VOROM reads heart rate, body weight, and (optionally) running power FROM Apple Health for use during workouts and in benchmarks. VOROM does NOT write workout data back to Apple Health unless you explicitly enable export. We do not use HealthKit data for marketing or advertising.

What is NOT collected

VOROM has no analytics, no ad SDKs, no tracking pixels. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or marketers. The app does not request your contacts, calendar, photos, or microphone. We do not use any of your data for marketing or advertising.

App Store privacy label

For Apple's App Privacy label, the current local-only version is designed as Data Not Collected: VOROM does not transmit your workout data, HealthKit-derived data, GPS routes, body weight, notes, app settings, or purchase-entitlement status to VOROM-operated servers or third-party analytics/advertising services. StoreKit purchases, Apple Health, Apple device backups, and Apple Watch system connectivity are handled by Apple according to Apple's own policies and your device settings.

Third-party services (sub-processors)

VOROM relies on Apple platform services. We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or a VOROM-operated backend in the current local-only version.

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — handles purchases, Lifetime licenses, subscriptions, cancellations, refunds, and purchase restoration. VOROM reads StoreKit entitlement status only to unlock paid features; we do not receive your payment card details.
  • Apple Health / HealthKit — if you grant permission, VOROM reads selected health and workout-related data from Apple Health for app features. You can change this permission in iOS Settings at any time.
  • Apple WatchConnectivity — transfers workout state between your iPhone and Apple Watch.

International data transfer

VOROM does not transfer your training database to VOROM-operated servers in the current local-only version. Apple may process App Store, StoreKit, HealthKit, and Apple Watch-related data according to Apple's own policies and your device settings.

Data retention

Training data remains on your device until you delete it, wipe the app's local data, or uninstall the app. VOROM does not keep a server-side copy for restore in the current local-only version. Apple may retain App Store purchase records according to Apple's terms and policies.

Deleting your data

Because there is no VOROM account or server-stored training database in the current local-only version, there is no server account to delete. You can delete workouts, plans, exercises, body-weight entries, and other local records inside the app. Deleting the app from your device removes the app's local data unless you previously exported it or your device backup preserves it.

Offline template sharing

When you share a template via VOROM's Offline Template Share feature:

  • The template content (exercises, sets, notes, etc.) is encoded directly into a private deep link URL.
  • VOROM does not receive, store, log, or transmit the template content. The data resides entirely within the URL string itself.
  • The link is sent through your own messaging app of choice (iMessage, WhatsApp, AirDrop, email, etc.) to recipient(s) you choose.
  • Only the people you send the link to can decode and import the template.
  • VOROM has no record, log, or copy of shares performed via this feature.

Template reports and content blocks

The current local-only version does not send template reports or template content to a VOROM backend. If you receive an objectionable or infringing template link from someone else, contact the sender or the messaging service used to send it. You can also email support@vorom.app with concerns, but VOROM may not be able to review or block content that exists only inside a private link we never received.

Export

Settings → Data → Export My Data generates a JSON + CSV snapshot of your training data. The export always returns your full unrestricted history regardless of tier. Because the current app is local-only, we strongly recommend exporting periodically and saving the file somewhere outside the app, such as iCloud Drive, Files, AirDrop, or email.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA / similar laws)

If you live in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, California, or any jurisdiction with similar data-protection laws, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or erase personal data. VOROM's Export My Data feature gives you a copy of the local data stored by the app. Because the current version does not maintain a VOROM account or server database for your training history, most access and deletion actions happen directly on your device. For questions, contact support@vorom.app.

Children

VOROM is not directed to children under 13 (under 16 in the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom).

Changes

If this policy changes, the app will show a banner on next launch with a summary and link to the new version. Continued use means acceptance.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email support@vorom.app.
